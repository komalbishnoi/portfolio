    Website Redesign - OAA (Ottawa Arts Association)
    - March 2019.

      Goal

      OAA(Ottawa Arts Association) website was lacking the artistic look, so there was a need to recreate the brand's overall look and feel.

      Problems

      • There was no basic layout in the different webpages.
      • The logo was too simple and did'nt relate to the brand.
      • Unequal spacing between coloumns.
      • Lack of color harmony.
      • No uniformity between elements and overall flat look.

      Discovery

      Feedback from various users was taken, about the website experience and asethetics.Research about other artistic organizations website was done to come up with a design direction.


      Solutions


    1. Color & Typography

      - Artistic look.
      - Vibrant colors balanced with neutral gradients adds liveliness to the theme.
      - Bold fonts for the headings and basic sans-serif font to the paragraphs.

    2. Content flow & Alignment

      - Content placed inside columns
      - Header, navbar are equally aligned.
      - Balance between images and text. read easily and visually appealing.

    3. Interface & Usability

      - Simplified Gallery is simplified by using medium sized tiles for every artist with their information on hover.
      - Reduced text clutter on the page.
      - Footer center aligned and relevant information and been added.
      - Consistent theme is maintained to visually and experientially improve the interface and usability of the website.

    4. Logo

      – last but not least, the logo which defines the persona of the brand was again too simple
      - Vibrant colors on floral pattern

    Website design concept - The Northern (local band)
    - April 2019.

      Goal

      To design a website for The northern Band to share their musical passion with audience and express themselves through lively and engaging music.

      Problems

      • No presnt website, to get inspiration of the brand appeal.
      • All Ui elemenst were created from scratch.

      Discovery

      Research was done about the dempgraphics and psychographics of the target audience who will visit the website, based on that, personas were created. Overall look and feel was kept in sync with the gothic theme as it was metallic music band. Feedback from various users was taken, about the website experience and asethetics. Research about other artistic organizations website was also done to come up with a design direction.


    1. Color & Typography

      - Gothic and punk look.
      - Neon colors balanced with deep tones.
      - Color theme is choosen according the colors of Aurora in the dark sky.

    2. Content flow & Alignment

      - Content placed inside columns
      -Separate sections for various information.
      -Bright text makes it easier to focus on important information.
      - Balance between images and text. read easily and visually appealing.

    3. Interface & Usability

      - Dedicated pages for each category of information.
      - Reduced text clutter on the pageS.
      - social icons are made bigger and noticable for fan and followers.
      - Consistent theme is maintained to visually and experientially improve the interface and usability of the website.

    4. Logo

      – last but not least, the logo which defines the persona of the brand is designed in a star shape surrounding the name of the band.
